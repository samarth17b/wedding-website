Schedule of Events

Saturday 14 November 2026

A full day of colour, food, and celebration — come as you are and stay as long as you like.

12:00 – 2:00 PM

Haldi

Let the celebrations begin! Join us for a joyful, laid-back haldi with our closest friends and family — no formal poojas, just love, laughter, and plenty of turmeric. Come smear the couple with haldi to your heart’s content (though we’d gently ask you to spare the bride’s face!). We’ll have a couple of mehendi artists on hand if you fancy some quick henna, and a hearty lunch will be served right through the afternoon.

Dress code: wear your sunniest shades — yellow, gold, or orange.

4:00 – 5:00 PM

High Tea

Take a breather and drift back in for high tea before the evening kicks off. Grab a bite, sip something warm, and soak up that gorgeous golden-hour light — perfect for a few pictures before the reception.

6:30 PM onwards

Reception

Get ready for an evening to remember! Vegetarian fare will be served on the lawn, while The Blue Room hosts non-vegetarian delights and a well-stocked bar for those who partake. Dinner winds down around 9:30–10 PM — and then the real fun begins: from 9:30 PM, The Blue Room turns into a dancefloor with our DJ spinning through the night. No sangeet, no performances — just all of us shaking a leg together! Drinks keep flowing in The Blue Room until around 10:30–11 PM.

Sunday 15 November 2026

The morning we tie the knot — the heart of the whole celebration.

8:30 – 9:30 AM

Breakfast

Ease into the big morning with a relaxed breakfast served on the lawn. Come fuel up before the ceremony!

10:15 AM

Muhurtham

The moment we’ve all been waiting for. Gather with us on the lawn as Ninisha and Samarth tie the knot, surrounded by the people they love most. We’d be so grateful to have you there as we say “I do.”

12:30 PM onwards

Lunch

Celebrate the newlyweds over a traditional South Indian banana-leaf feast, with a little northern fusion woven in. Come hungry — this one’s a treat!

Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

Is accommodation provided?

Yes — hotel rooms are available for guests who need them from Friday, 13 November, with breakfast at the hotel on Saturday morning. Priority goes to guests who told us they needed a room in our earlier Google Form.

What are the hotel check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 2 PM on Friday, 13 November. Check-out is expected around 12 PM, and we’ll keep a few late-checkout rooms where you can leave your luggage. Exact details will follow closer to the wedding weekend.

How do I get between the hotel and the venue?

We’re arranging a shuttle between the hotel and The Woodrose Club (just 2.8 km away). Planned shuttle times:

  • Saturday, 14 Nov: 12:00–2:30 PM (Haldi) and 6:30–11:00 PM (Reception)
  • Sunday, 15 Nov: 8:00 AM–2:00 PM (Muhurtham)

Prefer to travel on your own? Uber, Ola, and Rapido all work well here.

If I can only make one event, which should I come to?

The Reception on Saturday evening — we’d love to celebrate with you there.

Will there be a stage or photo line at the reception?

No traditional stage or photo queue. Just relax, eat, and mingle — our photographer will be capturing candids all evening, so you’ll still go home with lovely pictures.
